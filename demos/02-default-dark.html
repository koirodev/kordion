Emotion motherboard — the base of the dish, providing a foundation for the steps ahead.

Unity processor — to bring all the components together in harmony.

Memory of shared moments — for an extra touch of flavor and warmth.

Pour in a virtual broth of nostalgia to create a rich, multi-layered taste.

Activate the creativity modules — add a few scenarios for a touch of spice.

Stir in some inspiration drivers to create the perfect texture, adding water as needed to reach the ideal consistency.

Serve the soup in an old monitor for a unique presentation.

Garnish with fresh ideas and a dash of smiles, adding a bit more water if you prefer a lighter taste.

Enjoy this one-of-a-kind soup where technology and flavor come together in perfect harmony, with water bringing every component into a unified blend.

«Computer Leftover Patties» — an inventive way to repurpose old computer parts into a flavorful and crispy dish. We take the leftovers of obsolete tech and turn them into a delicious, high-tech snack.

Obsolete motherboard — the base of the patties, providing a strong foundation for the dish.

Old processor chips — to give the patties a crunchy texture and flavor with a hint of nostalgia.

Memory modules — to provide depth and richness to the patties, adding a touch of speed and efficiency.

Clean all the parts carefully, removing any dust and debris, preparing them for the mixing process.

Chop the motherboard and chips into smaller pieces to create a perfect texture for the patties.

Combine all the components — motherboard, chips, and memory — with a dash of creativity and a touch of nostalgia.

Shape the mixture into small patties, ensuring they hold together well. The motherboard provides a sturdy base while the chips give the right texture.

Fry the patties in a bit of oil until golden and crispy on both sides, ensuring the memory modules add the right crunch.

Sprinkle a touch of data dust (aka some seasoning) to enhance the flavor, blending the techy taste with savory goodness.

For a techy presentation, serve the patties on a circuit board plate, adding a final touch of geek chic.

Enjoy your Computer Leftover Patties, where technology and taste collide in a crispy, nostalgic treat!

«Whisky from Computer Cooling Liquid» is a unique blend of technology and distillation. Using the power of liquid cooling, this drink combines the efficiency of cooling systems with the art of alcohol creation. The result is a beverage that is both refreshing and mind-bending, combining the best of both worlds in a futuristic taste.

The base ingredient is the liquid used for cooling your computer's internal components. It's cool, it’s refreshing, but don't consume it directly!

Distilled water to cleanse the base and prepare it for flavoring.

Dark rum is added for complexity and depth, providing a rich flavor to complement the base.

In a large container, gently mix the cooling liquid with distilled water to purify and adjust the texture. This will serve as the drink's base.

Chill the mixture in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to enhance its crisp and refreshing taste.

Now, pour in the dark rum. The bold and robust flavor of the rum will mix beautifully with the cooling liquid's base.

Stir the mixture thoroughly to ensure that the rum is well integrated into the cooling liquid base.

Pour the chilled whisky into a glass and garnish with a thin slice of citrus for an added zing.

Enjoy this mind-bending blend where technology meets tradition, and the future of spirits is born!
